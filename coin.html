About AqsaCoin

AqsaCoin is a decentralized digital currency based on a strong Islamic ethos. It aims to promote ethical finance and support charitable projects.

Total Supply

The total supply of AqsaCoin is fixed at 500 million coins, with a mining reward of 3 coins every 24 hours.

Mining

Users can mine AqsaCoin by participating in the network and contributing to its validation process.
